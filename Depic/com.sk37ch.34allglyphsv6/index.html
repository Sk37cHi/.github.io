3 4 All [Glyphs v6]

An icon theme for iOS9x iOS8x and iOS7x. Welcome to the 6th (and most extensive) edition of the "3 4 All" glyph series. David and I have basicallly gone flat with this set. All gradients removed, and a mild 1px stroke has been added instead of a drop shadow, just to help define the edge of the white glyph. I hope to bring a well executed alternative to the existing detailed skeuomorphic icons, that you've come to know with 3 4 All.

This series of glyph icons has been made to utalise batch processing via Photoshop, and therefor does NOT require the use of Iconomatic (though this version is more suited towards the tweak). Many weeks have gone into the careful planning of the main template, and the possible scripting possibilities. The icons will be released in separate sets, and a detailed explanation of each version, and its options. v2, v3, v4, v5 mods, now available in Cydia.

Compatible with iPhone's running iOS8 & iOS7. Requires Winterboard. iP6+ support availabel soon.

 

::  Features ::

  • All Stock icons themed (iOS7/8/9 support).
  • Icons designed using solid Illustrator techniques.
  • Settings and NC icons, including iOS7 and 8x.
  • Over 325 apps themed including third party icons, with more to come.
  • A unique execution of theming on iOS7 (seprate iP6 Plus 180px icon output).
  • ClassicDock, Classic badge, and standard "masked" badge included.
  • Add-ons available separately, including UI, widgets, Iconomatic and more.
  • FolderIcons (available in Cydia).
  • Many updates to come.
