3 4 All [Zeppelin]

This is a package for use with the Zeppelin tweak in Cydia. Includes 2 hand drawn logo's (MOG, and Alien), Ai constructed robot, coffee cup and FaceTune face. Enjoy;)

 

::Instructions::

  • Requires Zeppelin tweak.
  • Apply your desired logo in Preferences -> Zeppelin.
  • Contains retina & non-retina logo's (with iP6+ support).
