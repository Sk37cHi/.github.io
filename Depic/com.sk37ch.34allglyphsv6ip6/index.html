3 4 All [Glyphs v6]

An icon theme for iPhone6 Plus for iOS9/8. Welcome to the 6th version of the glyph series, for the "3 4 All" theme. David and I have basicallly gone flat with this set. All gradients removed, and a mild 1px stroke has been added instead of a drop shadow, just to help define the edge of the white glyph. I hope to bring a well executed alternative to the existing detailed skeuomorphic icons, that you've come to know with 3 4 All.

This series of glyph icons has been made to utalise batch processing via Photoshop, and therefor does NOT require the use of Iconomatic (though this set is more suited towards the tweak). Many weeks have gone into the careful planning of the main template, and the possible scripting possibilities. The icons will be released in separate sets, and a detailed explanation of each version, and its options. The script allows for customised icon sets, inclusive of tailored NC and IconCache preference icons.

Compatible with iPhone6+ running iOS8/9. Requires Winterboard. For a vector resized and optimised version for iPad, please see "3 4 All [Glyphs][iPad]" in Cydia.

::  Features ::

  • All Stock icons themed (iOS8/9 support).
  • Settings and NC icons, iOS8/9.
  • Over 325 apps themed including third party icons, with more to come.
  • A unique execution of theming on iOS8 (seprate iP6 Plus 180px icon output).
  • Classic badge included.
  • McFly 5 Day forecast iWidgets available on the MMi forum.
  • Add-ons available separately, including UI, widgets, Iconomatic, and more.
  • Many updates to come.
