3 4 All

An icon theme for iOS8x and iOS7x. After many time consuming hours, I'm pleased to present the third in the "4 You" icon series.

When first introduced to iOS7, I drew up a few concepts, this being the flatter of the 2. Based on the "4 You 2" icon set, each icon has been remade to suit the iOS7 icon mask. This isn't just a simple port, most styles have been minimized to try and reach a comfortable balance beetween the old icons, and the new flat look of iOS7. All icons have been vector resized to suit the iPad retina display.

Compatibile with iPhone's running iOS8x and iOS7x. Requires Winterboard. For a vector resized and optimazed version for iPad, please see "3 4 All [iPad]" in Cydia. Not compatible with iPhone6+ (coming soon).

 

::  Features ::

  • Compatible with iOS7 through iOS8x
  • All Stock icons themed.
  • Settings and NC icons, including iOS8x iconcache.
  • Over 180 apps themed including third party icons, with more to come.
  • McFly 5 Day forecast iWidgets available on the MMi forum.
  • Add-ons availaible seprately, including Iconomatic skin, UI, Alt icons, and more.
  • Many updates to come.
