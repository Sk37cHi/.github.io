3 4 All [Glyphs v3]

An icon theme for iOS8x and iOS7x. Welcome to the third version of the glyph series, for the "3 4 All" theme. This time round, we have a slighly different look, with long shadows....I know, I did it... but slightly different. I hope to bring a well executed alternative to the existing detailed skeuomorphic icons, that you've come to know with 3 4 All.

This series of glyph icons has been made to utalise batch processing via Photoshop, and therefor does NOT require the use of Iconomatic. Many weeks have gone into the careful planning of the main template, and the possible scripting possibilities. The icons will be released in separate sets, and a detailed explanation of each version, and its options. The script allows for customised icon sets, inclusive of tailored NC and IconCache preference icons.

Compatible with iPhone's running iOS8 and iOS7. Requires Winterboard. For a vector resized and optimised version for iPad, please see "3 4 All [Glyphs][iPad]" in Cydia. iPhone6+ support comming soon, in a seprate package.

 

::  Features ::

  • iOS7 & 8 support (including iOS8.3).
  • All Stock icons themed.
  • Settings and NC icons, including iOS7.1x.
  • Over 325 apps themed including third party icons, with more to come.
  • A unique execution of theming on iOS8 (seprate iP6 Plus 180px icon output).
  • ClassicDock, and Classic badge included.
  • McFly 5 Day forecast iWidgets available on the MMi forum.
  • Add-ons available separately, including UI, widgets, and more.
  • Many updates to come.
