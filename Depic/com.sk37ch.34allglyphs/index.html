3 4 All [Glyphs]

An icon theme for iOS8x and iOS7x. Welcome to the glyph series for the "3 4 All" theme. I hope to bring a well executed alternative to the existing detailed skeuomorphic icons, that you've come to know with 3 4 All.

This series of glyph icons has been made to utalise batch processing via Photoshop, and therefor does NOT require the use of Iconomatic. Many weeks have gone into the careful planning of the main template, and the possible scripting possibilities. The icons will be released in separate sets, and a detailed explanation of each version, and its options. v2, v3, v4, v5, v6 mods, now available in Cydia.

Compatible with iPhone's running iOS7. Requires Winterboard. For a vector resized and optimised version for iPad, please see "3 4 All [Glyphs][iPad]" in Cydia.

 

::  Features ::

  • All Stock icons themed (iOS8.4 support).
  • Settings and NC icons, including iOS7 and 8x.
  • Over 325 apps themed including third party icons, with more to come.
  • A unique execution of theming on iOS8 (seprate iP6 Plus 180px icon output).
  • ClassicDock, and Classic badge included.
  • McFly 5 Day forecast iWidgets available on the MMi forum.
  • Add-ons available separately, including UI, widgets, and more.
  • Custom FolderIcons available on the MMi Thread.
  • Many updates to come.
