3 4 All [Zeppelin]

 

This is a package for use with the Zeppelin tweak in Cydia. Includes 2 hand drawn logo's (MOG, and Alien), and a 3rd Ai constructed robot. Enjoy;)

 

 

::Instructions::

 

  • Requires Zeppelin tweak.

  • Apply your desired logo in Preferences -> Zeppelin.

  • Contains retina & non-retina logo's (with iP6+ support).
